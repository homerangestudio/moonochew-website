## Photo Guidelines

### Technical Requirements
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **Minimum Resolution:** As specified above
- **File Size:** Under 5MB each (will be optimized for web)

### Style Guidelines
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, golden hour best
- **Tone:** Warm, authentic, Australian rural
- **Focus:** Sharp on main subject
- **Composition:** Follow rule of thirds where appropriate

### Naming Convention
Use descriptive names with hyphens:
- `andrew-humphreys-portrait.jpg`
- `hero-landscape-golden-hour.jpg`
- `installation-step-1-unfold.jpg`

---

## How to Upload

1. Log into TinaCMS admin at `yoursite.com/admin`
2. Navigate to Media Library
3. Upload photos to appropriate folders
4. Update page content to reference new images
